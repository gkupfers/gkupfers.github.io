current problem:
students are struggling to achieve
balance between health and academics at CMU.
many are constantly stressed, unhappy, and unaware
of how they can use food to improve their wellbeing.

research question:
“how might we improve nutritional literacy
among Carnegie Mellon students
in regards to both on-campus and off-campus dining ?”



carnegie kitchen is:
a nutrition app that subtly
encourages behavior changes
by integrating habit-building nudges
with practical dining functions.

stakeholder map of relationships b/w CMU facilities and different types of student eaters
